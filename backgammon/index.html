Rules / notes

Standard backgammon rules. Black moves from point 24→1 (top→bottom-left); White from point 1→24 (bottom→top-right). Bar pieces must re-enter before other moves. Bearing off begins once all 15 checkers are in the home board (points 1–6 for White, 19–24 for Black). Doubling cube not included.

Exit game?
Your progress will be lost.
