Quoridor

Pass-and-play · 2–4 players
Players:
Red moves first. Reach the opposite side to win.
Rules

Move: Move your pawn one step (orthogonally, or jump over an adjacent pawn). Wall: Place a wall (2 cells long) to block paths — you have 10 walls each (5 in 4-player). Win: Be the first to reach the opposite row. Walls must never completely block any player from reaching their goal.

Exit game?
Your progress will be lost.
