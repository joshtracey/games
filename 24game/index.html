How to play

Use each of the 4 numbers exactly once with +, −, ×, ÷ and brackets to make 24.

Operator precedence applies (× ÷ before + −), so use brackets where needed. Example: (3+1)×6 or 8÷(3-8÷8).

In Race mode the first player to submit a correct answer scores a point, then new cards are dealt.
