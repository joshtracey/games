Rules — Kalah

Setup: Each player owns the 6 pits on their side and the store to their right. Stones start in each pit.

Move: Pick any of your pits with stones. Distribute stones one-by-one counter-clockwise, dropping one in each pit and your own store — skip the opponent's store.

Extra turn: If your last stone lands in your store, take another turn.

Capture: If your last stone lands in an empty pit on your side, capture it and all stones in the opposite pit — all go to your store.

End: When one player's pits are all empty, the other player captures all remaining stones on their side. Most stones in store wins.
